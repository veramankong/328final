Residential and Commercial Duct Cleaning

We use the industrial leading portable power-vac to clean your system. Compared to others, who use less powerful vacuums. Such as those used in carpet cleaning.

When using our gas portable vacuum in conjunction with the patented Viper Whip System, West Coast Power-Vac can effectively remove all debris from a residential system in 2 hours or less. Our methods are clean, safe, reliable and follow NADCA (National Air Duct Cleaning Association) regulations.

West Coast Power-Vac Call today and let us get your ducts off dust!

Air Duct Cleaning

Air whip agitation through every heat vent and air return. While under negative air pressure from a gas powered vacuum. To remove harmful dust, pollens, allergens, and construction debris.

Furnace Cleaning

Clean the air handler to free it of dust accumulation, we also can clean the burners and heat exchanger (gas furnace) as well as the coil from A/C or heat pump.


Dryer Vent Cleaning

Clean the entire length of run from the exterior (where it vents out) to the back of the dryer. We also do dryer vent replacement and repairs.
